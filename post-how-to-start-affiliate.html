How to Start Affiliate Marketing as a Beginner

Published: 2025

Affiliate marketing is one of the easiest ways to start earning online. Start by picking a niche, join affiliate programs (like Amazon, Jumia, or others), create helpful content that solves problems, and add your affiliate links inside helpful posts.

Quick steps

  1. Choose a niche you understand.
  2. Sign up for affiliate programs.
  3. Create helpful articles and reviews.
  4. Promote on social media and SEO.
